AI Research Scientist

I am an AI Research Scientist from Lomé, Togo, currently pursuing my Ph.D. in Computer Science at Florida Institute of Technology. My research focuses on deep neural networks, reinforcement learning, and their applications in robotics and autonomous systems.

Previously, I worked on autonomous racing systems at the Indy Autonomous Challenge, developing perception and planning algorithms for high-speed autonomous vehicles. I am passionate about building AI systems that can solve complex, real-world challenges and empower humanity.

My work bridges the worlds of Entrepreneurship and Research, applying rigorous scientific methods alongside iterative product development. I believe in the power of AI to transform industries and improve lives.

Research Interests

  • Deep Reinforcement Learning — Policy optimization, model-based RL, and sample efficiency
  • Autonomous Systems — Perception, planning, and control for robots and vehicles
  • Neural Network Representations — Understanding and improving learned representations
  • Imitation Learning — Learning from demonstrations and human feedback
  • Humanoid Robotics — Locomotion, manipulation, and human-robot interaction
  • AI Agents — Building intelligent agents for digital and physical environments

Open to Opportunities

I am actively seeking research scientist, research engineer, and ML engineer positions in industry or academia. Particularly interested in roles involving autonomous systems, robotics, or foundation models.
