Who I am

AI Research Scientist from Togo 🌍.

I am an AI Research Scientist originating from Lome, Togo. Currently pursuing my Ph.D. in Computer Science, I focus on deep neural networks, reinforcement learning, and their applications in robotics and autonomous systems. My goal is to advance the state of AI to solve complex, real-world challenges.

Research Areas

Advancing Intelligent Systems 🤖.

My research focuses on creating autonomous agents capable of complex decision making in dynamic environments. I work at the intersection of:

AI Models & Algorithms

Developing novel architectures and algorithms for deep learning, focusing on efficiency, generalization, and robustness.

Digital Agents

Building intelligent software agents that can perceive, reason, and act to assist humans in digital environments.

Humanoid Robots

Researching control and perception for humanoid platforms to enable natural interaction and manipulation in human-centric spaces.

Autonomous Vehicles

Designing perception and planning systems for safe and efficient autonomous navigation in complex traffic scenarios.
