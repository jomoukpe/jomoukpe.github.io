I am a technology enthusiast. In my spare time, I hack on epic AI projects. Here is how I normally operate:

1

Define

Before every project, I systematically define the objectives of the project. Well-defined project objectives allow for testable, verifiable, and actionable results.

2

Design

Next, I dive into the design, brainstorming architectures, structures, components of the project. Through quick analysis, I try eliminate as many potential issues as possible before building.

3

Build

With a good-enough design, I start building, one module at a time. That usually means I formulate algorithms, architect models, and write code. I iterate many times building, measuring, and learning, until I obtain the MVP.

4

Launch

I deploy the MVP quickly for the world to see and critique. Based on user feedback, I iterate further until the product provides the intended value and the users love it.
